# Superior Environmental Solutions

**Location:** Cincinnati, OH

## Overview

Superior Environmental Solutions (SES) is a leading provider of environmental, industrial, emergency response, and product destruction services primarily serving the Midwestern and Southeastern United States. SES has over 500 employees and serves over 500 customers across end markets such as agriculture, food processing, metals, utilities, and chemicals from more than 25 locations in Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Michigan, Wisconsin, Iowa, California, Kentucky, Tennessee, and North Carolina.
