# Envoy Global Expands Reach in Germany via Acquisition of Offer & Mastmann by Corporate Immigration Partners, P.C.

**July 1st, 2025**

— Envoy Global's affiliate law firm, Corporate Immigration Partners, P.C., has acquired Offer & Mastmann, a leading German immigration law firm based in Frankfurt. —

CHICAGO and FRANKFURT, Germany, July 1, 2025 /PRNewswire/ — Envoy Global, the leading global corporate immigration services provider, today announced the acquisition of Offer & Mastmann Rechtsanwälte PartG, a premier immigration law firm based in Frankfurt am Main, Germany, by its U.S. affiliate, Corporate Immigration Partners, P.C. (CIPPC). This strategic acquisition further strengthens Envoy Global's ability to offer top-tier global immigration services across the world to its clients.

Founded in 2005, Offer & Mastmann has built a strong reputation for excellence in German employment-based immigration law, serving multinational corporations and individuals with a focus on EU Intra-Company Transferee (ICT) cards, Local Hire Work & Residence Permit categories, EU Blue Cards and Schengen C visas for short-term assignments, business travel and trainings. The firm's deep local expertise and client-first approach align seamlessly with Envoy Global's mission to provide innovative, tech-enabled immigration services worldwide.

## Envoy Global's Global Expansion

Envoy Global has made several strides in global expansion since 2022. The company acquired IBN Immigration Solutions in South Africa, Kenya and Namibia in early 2025, leading UK firm Smith Stone Walters in October of 2024 and recently expanded into Saudi Arabia, France, Spain and the Netherlands. They acquired UAE-based Sesam Immigration in August of 2023. Through their exclusive affiliation with Corporate Immigration Partners, P.C., Envoy Global is able to offer innovative, high-quality legal services in the U.S. and now, Germany.

## About Offer & Mastmann Rechtsanwälte PartG

Founded in 2005, Offer & Mastmann is the first German law firm to specialize exclusively in corporate immigration law. Recognized by Who's Who Legal as a leading provider in the field since 2009, the firm is regularly consulted by institutions engaged in policy-making and legislative development. Legal insights are shared through HR webinars and numerous contributions to renowned journals; firm leaders and attorneys are frequent authors of the leading commentary on the German Employment Regulation (Beschäftigungsverordnung) and further commentaries and specialist books. Based in Frankfurt am Main, the heart of the German financial industry, Offer & Mastmann provides highly accessible legal services. The firm's attorneys also lecture at Beck Akademie and Deutsche Anwaltakademie (DAA), contributing to the advancement of legal education in the field. Bettina and Gabriele have again been recently acknowledged by Best Lawyers/Handelblatt.
