# Quirch

**Location:** Miami, FL

## Overview

Quirch Foods is a leading distributor, importer and exporter of protein and Hispanic food products primarily to independent and ethnic grocers in the U.S., Puerto Rico and Latin American export markets. Quirch's extensive portfolio of owned and licensed brands includes Panamei Seafood®, Mambo® and Chiquita Frozen®. Quirch operates distribution facilities in the U.S. and Puerto Rico.
