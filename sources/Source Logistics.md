# Source Logistics

**Location:** Houston, TX

## Overview

Source Logistics ("Source") is a provider of value-added warehousing, distribution and fulfillment third-party logistics (3PL) services to the consumer goods sector with a focus on Hispanic food and beverage brands. The Company is based in Houston, TX, with facilities across the U.S., representing over 5.0 million square feet of space in Texas, California, Illinois, New Jersey, and Oregon.
