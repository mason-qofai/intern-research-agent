# Kymera International

**Location:** Research Triangle Park, NC

## Overview

Kymera International is a leading, global developer and manufacturer of advanced specialty materials and high-performance surface coatings. Focused on growth and furthering the technology of material science, Kymera is shaping the future of manufacturing and infusing innovation into every particle produced. Complimented by a vast portfolio of capabilities, Kymera is a leading material and service supplier across a diverse range of end-markets, including aerospace and defense, automotive, building and construction, chemical, industrial, electronics and semiconductors, medical and energy.
