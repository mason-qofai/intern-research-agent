# DME Express

**Location:** Woodlands, TX

## Overview

DME Express is a provider of durable medical equipment and logistics services to hospice providers across the United States. Founded in 2006, the company delivers a broad suite of equipment, including beds, respiratory equipment, mobility products and support surfaces, through recurring per-patient-day contractual arrangements. DME Express differentiates itself through its high-touch service model, dense operating footprint and proprietary technology platform, which enables rapid, reliable delivery and service in time-sensitive care settings. Headquartered in The Woodlands, Texas, the company serves hospice providers and patients through a network of warehouse locations across multiple states.
