# Palladium Heritage Strategy

*We are a private equity firm that's proud to take our own path.*

## Overview

**Palladium Equity Partners** is a middle market private equity firm founded in 1997. We seek to invest in and grow companies in partnership with founders and experienced management teams by providing capital, strategic guidance and operational oversight.

**Palladium Heritage** is a Joint Venture with Palladium, focused on lower middle market transactions.

## Strategy Focus

The Palladium Heritage Strategy targets lower middle-market transactions in the Business-to-Business and Industrial Sectors.

## Heritage Strategy Target Investment Criteria

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Key Sectors** | Business-to-Business and Industrial |
| **Target Company Size** | $3 - $12M in EBITDA (no minimum for add-ons) |
| **Geographic Focus** | North America-focused headquarters or significant operations |
| **Investment Size** | $10 - $50M invested capital. Majority control or significant minority |
| **Transaction Types / Stage** | Entrepreneur-backed growth / transitions, management buyouts and corporate divestitures. Comfortable and experienced with complexity |

## Heritage Strategy Investment Themes

### PRODUCTS
Component & Niche Manufacturing • Test & Inspection Equipment • Pump / Valve / Flow Control • B2B / OEM Sporting Goods • Food / Ag Chemicals, Ingredients, B2B products • Food / Ag Equipment or Packaging • Transportation Components

### SERVICES
Transportation & Logistics Services • Infrastructure Maintenance • Specialty Outsourced Services • Compliance / Business Processing • Training • Value Added Distributors • Test & Inspection Services

## Key Differentiators

Heritage focuses on lower middle-market transactions with significant operational complexity and opportunities for value creation through strategic growth, operational improvements, and strategic add-on acquisitions in the B2B and Industrial sectors.
