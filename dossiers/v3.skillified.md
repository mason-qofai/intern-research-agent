## 1. Firm Profile

*Not independently verified, and not treated as a sourced claim: press materials from mid-2025 reference AUM figures rising from roughly $3.0B to $3.5B alongside platform-count growth (230→240 businesses) over the course of 2025 — this trend was described in the intake profile narrative but no individual dated press release was assigned a claim ID or confidence rating by the confidence-scorer, so it is noted here for context only and should not be relied upon as a rated fact.*

### Fund Strategy
- **Flagship (middle market):** $50–150M of equity capital into control-oriented transactions (LBO/MBO, majority or minority positions) in U.S. companies with $10–75M EBITDA *(firm-profiler-005, High)*. Fund V, a 2018-vintage vehicle, raised $1.6 billion *(firm-profiler-007, High)*. Palladium Equity Partners VI, L.P. is an active successor vehicle; its Luxembourg feeder (Fund VI Lux) was formalized under SFDR Article 8 in 2024 *(firm-profiler-008, High)*. **Gap:** the exact size and vintage year of Fund VI itself are not disclosed in any available source.
- **Palladium Heritage (lower middle market):** $10–50M of invested capital into companies with $3–12M EBITDA (no minimum for add-ons), headquartered or with significant operations in North America, taking majority control or significant minority positions *(firm-profiler-006, High)*.

Since 1997, Palladium has made 41 platform investments and completed more than 200 add-on acquisitions, averaging more than 4 add-ons per portfolio company *(firm-profiler-013, High)*, and has acquired more than 230 businesses in total across its platforms *(firm-profiler-014, High)*.

### Geography Focus
The Flagship strategy's geographic focus is the United States, while the Heritage strategy targets companies headquartered or with significant operations in North America *(firm-profiler-009, Medium — comparative synthesis across two source documents, not a single-source restatement)*.

### Sector Focus
- Flagship key industries: Consumer, Healthcare, Industrials, Services *(firm-profiler-010, High)*.
- Heritage key sectors: Business-to-Business and Industrial *(firm-profiler-011, High)*.
- Cross-cutting theme: Palladium has invested approximately $1.5 billion in the U.S. Hispanic market since 2000, and roughly 60% of its portfolio companies are U.S. Hispanic businesses *(firm-profiler-012, High)*.
